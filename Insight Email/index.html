Here's how a website project usually works:
1 Content Strategy
An initial call or meeting will be set up to discuss what you're looking for. During the meeting I will be taking notes before getting into our Strategy Session. The strategy session will include a deep dive into the goals you have for your website, what success looks like, create key points on what to tackle, coordinate a visual direction, and plan out the overall content and structure of the site.
2 Wire Framing
At this point I will structure the framework of the website and together we will collaborate to refine and adjust the layout and content architecture until we are satisfied with the result. This is a blue print stage of the website, no final design choices will be done here (no fonts, colors etc.).
3 Design
Now the fun part. With the decisions made during our research and planning from the Strategy Session, we will begin designing your website. Once we're pleased with the aesthetic of the site, we can go into development.
4 Development
Webflow will be used to create the website. As I am created the website, I will be sending you links to view progress, these links will be subdomains (for example www.arinzeonochie.subdomain.com). This means we can test the site before it goes live. The website will be fully responsive, this means it will adapt to fit all screen sizes. This will be available for you to view throughout the process.
Once we've finished with all of the steps above, we will have a meeting where I will transfer the site over to you and show you how to edit it on your own. Trust me, it's simple. Then, you're good to go! I am only an email away if any issues occur.
